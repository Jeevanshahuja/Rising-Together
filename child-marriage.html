Child Marriage in Salampur Gujjar

Child marriage is a significant issue in many rural areas, including Salampur Gujjar. The practice has devastating consequences on young girls, cutting short their education, health, and overall well-being. It is crucial for the community to stand against this harmful practice.

The Impact of Child Marriage

  • Health Risks: Early pregnancies and childbirth can lead to serious health complications for young girls.
  • Loss of Education: Child brides are often forced to drop out of school, limiting their future opportunities.
  • Social and Economic Consequences: Child marriage perpetuates poverty and inequality, as girls are often excluded from economic opportunities.

How We Can Prevent It

Awareness programs, government schemes, and community support are essential in preventing child marriage. We must advocate for education, legal protections, and safe spaces for young girls.
