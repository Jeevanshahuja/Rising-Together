Healthcare & Medical Facilities in Salampur Gujjar

Access to quality healthcare is one of the biggest challenges in Salampur Gujjar. Many people face long travel distances to reach the nearest healthcare facility, and essential services are often limited. We aim to highlight the importance of accessible and affordable healthcare for all.
