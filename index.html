Welcome to Rising Together

Rising Together is a digital platform dedicated to addressing the critical social issues faced by the community of Salampur Gujjar. From combating child marriage to improving healthcare, hygiene, and sanitation, our goal is to create awareness and drive positive change in the village.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the people of Salampur Gujjar by providing resources, sharing success stories, and connecting individuals to the help they need. We aim to uplift the community by tackling the key challenges head-on.

Get Involved

We believe in the power of collective action. You can contribute by sharing knowledge, spreading awareness, volunteering, or donating to causes that impact the community. Together, we can make a difference.
