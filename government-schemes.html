Government Schemes for Salampur Gujjar

The government offers several schemes to improve the lives of people in rural areas like Salampur Gujjar. These schemes focus on health, education, sanitation, and financial aid. Unfortunately, many people are unaware of these benefits.

Available Schemes

  • Beti Bachao Beti Padhao: A scheme that promotes the education and welfare of girls, aiming to prevent child marriage.
  • Swachh Bharat Mission: A cleanliness campaign aimed at improving sanitation and waste management in villages.
  • Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana: A health insurance scheme that provides financial protection to families in case of illness.
